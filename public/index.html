Policies

A list of policies to be aware of.

Cancellation/Rescheduling Policy Please cancel or reschedule your appointment at least 24 hours in advance. If you cancel or reschedule anytime after that, you are required to pay an immediate fee of $20 before rebooking. Failing to pay within 48 hours will result in being PROHIBITED from rebooking at Muscle Melting Massage. Failure to show up for an appointment without any notice within the appointment time frame will require you to pay the full price of the massage.
